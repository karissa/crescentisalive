bio

Crescent Ulmer is a singer-songwriter from Indianapolis. She draws from many influences and genres. Stretching from hip-hop and folk, through jazz, r and b, indie rock, hardcore, pop, world, and punk rock, she pulls it all together to create a full and sweet acoustic sound that is refreshing and interesting to a wide and all inclusive audience. She sites Blink 182, Lauryn Hill, Dashboard Confessional, Jeff Magnum, and Buddy Wakefield as some of her greater influences.
